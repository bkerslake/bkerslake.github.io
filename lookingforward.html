Looking Forward

SafeBoat, Inc.

September 2021-Current

As of late September, 2021, I'm laying out the groundwork for my new personal project: SafeBoat, a neural-network powered ocean navigation application, which aims to use artificial intelligence to create data-rich navigation software for boaters. My primary target audience for it is the commercial shipping/fishing industry, where it'll plot a "best" route for ships based on thousands of dynamic variables including active storms, fuel levels, port congestion, and more.
